FAQ

What is endpoint management?
It’s the process of keeping devices patched, monitored, and configured consistently so problems happen less often and are faster to fix.
Do you support both Windows and Mac?
Yes. Support depends on device type and the tools involved. We’ll confirm scope during triage.
Is this for businesses only?
No. Individuals with one critical machine can also benefit from consistent patching and baseline security.
