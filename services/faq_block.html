FAQ

Do you work with businesses and individuals?
Yes. We support both businesses and individuals in Kankakee County. The scope and pricing depend on device count and urgency.
Do you offer remote support?
Yes. Many issues can be handled remotely. If on-site is needed, we will tell you up front.
How do you price work?
We scope first. You get a clear plan and pricing before we start billable work.
What do you need from me to start?
A brief description of the issue, your device type or website URL, and the best time to reach you.
